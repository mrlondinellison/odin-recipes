Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna has been one of my favorite foods since i was very young. Pastas, meats, cheeses and sauces are just so interchangeable and customizeable that the possibilities can be truly endless with the number of variations of this recipe you could create.

Lasagnas are a notoriously time consuming dish to make, but not to worry, this recipe will give you step by step instructions to be on your way to making the best lasagna your family has ever tasted!


Ingredients:

  • Lagagna Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Beef
  • Garlic
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna.
  2. Reduce the sauce.
  3. Brown the beef.
  4. Saute the garlic
  5. Melt the butter
  6. Combine!
